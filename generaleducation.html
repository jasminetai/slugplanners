General Education


At UCSC, all undergraduate students must complete a set of general education (GE) requirements to graduate. While classes taken to complete a major or minor encourage depth of learning in one discipline, GE requirements serve to promote a broader, balanced knowledge base. Plan well to ensure that all of your GEs are fulfilled!
